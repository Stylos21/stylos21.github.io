about me.

Hello! My name is Joshua Saji and I am currently a high school senior based in Maryland. For the 2024-2025 academic school year, I will be attending the University of Wisconsin - Madison as a computer science major.

My interest in programming started when I was just ten years old on ROBLOX. I wanted to learn how to make my own game, and from there, I downloaded ROBLOX's respective game editor, "ROBLOX Studio". Eight years later, I have dabbled into numerous fields of computer programming, from video game development, to web design.

Right now, I am specifically interested in deep learning and artificial intelligence. I have made a few projects incorporating AI models, and I am continuing to learn more about the mathematics behind the plethora of models in the field. Right now, my GitHub contains a lot of rudimentary/incomplete work, but that's mainly because since the end of the Coronavirus pandemic I have been relatively inconsistent in programming. However, I hope to advance my skills and make more complex projects combining web development and machine learning. Stay tuned for the next few months - I have too many project ideas and I cannot wait to (hopefully, within reason) develop them!

Some other things that I like to do other than programming: playing basketball & watching the NBA, working out, watching movies/TV shows, reading, hanging out with friends & listening to music.
